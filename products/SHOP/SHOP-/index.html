SHOP(Simulation Hydrodynamique OPérationnelle)

Overview

SHOP (Simulation Hydrodynamique OPérationnelle) is an operational hydrodynamic modeling tool which can produce numerical analyses and simulations of various parameters of interest for the St. Lawrence river and its tributaries.

Current version: 1.0.0

What is the spatial resolution and coverage of this data?

The analyses are produced for the portion of the St. Lawrence found between Montréal and Trois-Rivières.
